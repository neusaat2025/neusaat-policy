Child Safety Standards

Kongu Thirumana Maalai is committed to protecting children and strictly prohibits any form of child sexual abuse, exploitation, or harmful content.

Zero Tolerance Policy

Our platform enforces a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Any content, behavior, or user found to be engaging in or promoting CSAE will be immediately removed and permanently banned.

Age Restriction

Kongu Thirumana Maalai is intended only for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly allow minors to create accounts on the platform.

Content Monitoring & Reporting

Users can report suspicious profiles or content directly through the app or by contacting us via email. All reports are reviewed promptly.

Enforcement & Legal Compliance

We take appropriate action, including account termination and reporting to relevant law enforcement authorities, when CSAE violations are identified.
